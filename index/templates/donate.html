At Regina Community Fridge, the safety and well-being of our community is of extreme importance. For that reason, we have put together a donation guide in order to ensure everyone is on the same page.

Monetary donations

Donating money to the Regina Community Fridge allows us to purchase more supplies to keep the fridge running. If you have a donation of this kind, you can Donate to our GoFundMe

If you have a monetary donation in another form, Feel free to reach out to us and we will be in touch.

Food

Do Donate

  • Dairy and alternatives (milk, butter, yogurt, soy milk, coconut milk, margarine etc.)
  • Fresh fruits and vegetables.
  • Non-alcoholic drinks (water, juice, Gatorade, etc.)
  • Frozen meals for the freezer. (Frozen pizza, TV dinners, bread, soup, etc.)
  • Items that do not have to be frozen for the pantry (Canned goods, baby food, cereal, salt, sugar, oil, etc.)
  • Single-serving snacks such as granola bars (If expiry date is visible on the product)
  • Prepared meals if you are a business and/or have your food certification.

    • Please note that all items must be sealed, and an expiration date must be visible.

Don't Donate

  • Alcohol
  • Home-cooked meals
  • Rotten food
  • Opened food.

If you are a business and would like to make regular donations, Get in touch.

Non-food Items

Do Donate

  • Pads and tampons
  • Toothpaste, toothbrushes
  • Soap, body wash, shampoo
  • Hand sanitizer
  • Medical masks

    • Don't Donate

  • Opened or used items

Pets

Do Donate

  • Unopened pet food

Don't Donate

  • Stale or opened pet food
