Our mission

According to Global News, almost 20% of Saskatchewan experienced food insecurity in 2023, and that number is only growing. Our mission is to decrease that number by providing our community in Regina with a decentralized and easily accessible way to meet their basic human right of food.

What we do

We run a community fridge located in north central Regina. Here, you are free to take what you need at any time of day. All that we ask is that you help when you can. This can be something as simple as cleaning up boxes around the fridge or something as advanced as holding a large fundraiser.
